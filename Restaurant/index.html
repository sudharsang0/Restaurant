Welcome To ebees Restaurant

Three decades ago, a small sweet shop was started by Late K.S. Thirupathi Raja, in the metropolis of Chennai. This same shop has now flowered into multiple branches that dot the length and breadth of Chennai as well as its neighbouring cities.

The A2B family is synonymous with a commitment to quality and a high degree of professionalism by using state of the art equipment in the manufacture of sweets, savouries, snacks and myriad varieties of Indian food.
